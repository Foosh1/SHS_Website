Concrete Services

SHS provides a large variety of concrete services and product options to make your project become a reality.

  • Concrete Flat Work (Driveways, Patios, Sidewalks, Curbs and Engineered Slabs)

    • Concrete can be finished in many differnt styles and colours and we provide the options for you to customize your project

    • Broom Finish- Simple finish where a broom is dragged across the concrete to give it a grippier finish
    • Stamped Finish- Stamped patterns that can be used to imitate the look of Stone, Wood, Tiles etc.
    • Exposed Aggregate Finish- A special peastone concrete mix that shows off the stones in the concrete
    • Polished Finish- Machine Polished to be smooth
    • Coloured Concrete
  • Concrete Sealing
  • Concrete Foundations (Footings, Walls and Slabs)
  • Fencing
  • Rock Retaining Walls(Natural Stone, Armor Stone and Precast Concrete Stone)
